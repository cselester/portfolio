Custovista : AI-Powered Customer Insightsarrow_outward

Custovista is an interactive AI-powered application built on Hugging Face Spaces that enables businesses to gain real-time customer insights from their data. The project leverages multi-modal agentic AI and visualization techniques to analyze customer interactions, detect sentiment, and highlight emerging trends. The goal behind Custovista was to create a lightweight yet powerful tool for organizations that want to understand customer behavior without investing in complex enterprise solutions. With its simple interface and intuitive visualizations, Custovista makes it easier for teams to track satisfaction levels, identify pain points, and make data-driven decisions. By combining accessibility, explainability, and scalability, the project demonstrates how multi-modal AI can be democratized for customer analytics, empowering businesses of all sizes to act on customer feedback effectively.

FraudGuard : Fraud Stops Here arrow_outward

During HackHound 3.0, a 24-hour offline hackathon held at SRM-NCR Campus, I developed a lightweight fraud detection system aimed at addressing the growing issue of digital fraud. The motivation stemmed from the observation that most existing fraud detection systems are either too complex or financially inaccessible, especially for small businesses and startups. While larger organizations may have the resources to deploy advanced AI-based security solutions, smaller players often lack the infrastructure or technical expertise to do the same. Our solution was designed to be simple, fast, and easy to integrate, offering real-time detection of suspicious transactions without the overhead of large-scale systems. By focusing on accessibility, cost-effectiveness, and ease of use, our product empowers small enterprises to take the first step toward securing their digital operations with AI-driven insights.
